# Provisional Talk Outline From Builder

This is not a required presentation flow. It is the rough structure that was captured in the repo, preserved as context.

## 1. The MarTech explosion — 200 to 15,000

- Duration: 2 min
- Key points:
  - Scott Brinker landscape growth
  - 200 tools in 2011 → 15,000+ in 2024
  - The "hypertail" concept
- Speaker notes: Opening hook. Show the growth chart. Make the audience feel the scale of the problem.

## 2. The cost inversion — code is cheap now

- Duration: 5 min
- Key points:
  - AI coding assistants changed economics
  - Building a platform in 2 months vs 2 years
  - The 1% vs 99% adoption gap
- Speaker notes: Core thesis. Code used to be expensive and requirements cheap. Now it's flipped.

## 3. What this breaks — the SaaS model

- Duration: 5 min
- Key points:
  - Why pay monthly for something you can build?
  - The bundling/unbundling cycle
  - Custom > generic for many use cases
- Speaker notes: Implications for the industry. SaaS as we know it is under pressure.

## 4. The new framework — build vs buy

- Duration: 5 min
- Key points:
  - Decision matrix
  - Complexity vs uniqueness
  - The "vibe coding" phenomenon
  - Billions of custom apps prediction
- Speaker notes: Practical framework for the audience. When does building make sense?

## 5. What developers become — orchestrators

- Duration: 3 min
- Key points:
  - From writing code to defining requirements
  - The product thinking shift
  - Context is the moat
- Speaker notes: The human element. Developers aren't replaced, they're elevated.

## 6. The enterprise trap — guardrailing vs adopting

- Duration: 5 min
- Key points:
  - Enterprise paralysis examples
  - The 1% adoption stat
  - Fear vs opportunity framing
- Speaker notes: Challenge the enterprise audience. Are you innovating or just protecting?

## 7. What's next — agents, context, self-building company

- Duration: 5 min
- Key points:
  - Agentic workflows
  - Context and memory as infrastructure
  - The self-building company concept
  - Call to action
- Speaker notes: Forward-looking close. Paint the vision of where this is heading.
